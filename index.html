The Band

Messi Suarez Neymar

Commonly known by the acronym 'MSN', the trio were sensational during their short, three-year period at Camp Nou, winning two La Liga titles, a Champions League title, three Copa del Rey titles and the Club World Cup. The mix of South American flair, relentless goalscoring and a tireless work ethic made 'MSN' strike fear into the hearts of every defence in Spain and in Europe. From the start of the 2014/15 season to the end of 2016/17 when Neymar left for Paris Saint-Germain, they scored a ludicrous total of 363 goals. What is notable about how good the trio were, is that they were extremely unselfish towards each other.
